What is PM-KISAN?
Full Name: Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi Yojana
Launched: February 2019 (effective from December 1, 2018)
Funded by: Central Government — 100% Union exchequer funding
Purpose: Direct income support to landholding farmer families to help with farming expenses, input costs (seeds, fertilisers, pesticides), and household needs.

Benefit

Total: ₹6,000 per year
Paid in 3 installments of ₹2,000 each
Payment method: Direct to Aadhaar-linked bank account via DBT (Direct Benefit Transfer)
No middlemen — money goes directly to bank account

Who is Eligible?
Basic requirements (ALL must be true):

Owns cultivable land in their name (as per official land records)
Land must have been in their name as of February 1, 2019 (cut-off date)
Has a valid Aadhaar number
Has an active bank account seeded/linked with Aadhaar
Has a unique, active mobile number

Beneficiary unit definition: The scheme treats a family (husband + wife + minor children) as ONE unit. Only one payment per family — not per individual.
Age: No minimum or maximum age limit. Age is optional during registration.
Land size: No minimum or maximum land size. All landholding farmers are eligible (was only small/marginal farmers originally, expanded in 2019 to all).
Geography: Pan-India. Applicable in all states and UTs including North East states (with region-specific verification for communal landholding areas).

Special Land Ownership Rules
Cut-off date rule: Land must be in the farmer's name as per records dated on or before February 1, 2019.

Land bought/gifted/transferred after Feb 1, 2019 = NOT eligible
If a registered farmer sells/gifts their entire land after Feb 1, 2019 = they lose eligibility immediately

Inheritance exception: If the original landowner dies, the surviving spouse or minor children who inherit the land ARE eligible — the Feb 1, 2019 cut-off does NOT apply to inheritance cases. State must update land records (mutation) and new heir must submit a fresh self-declaration.
Transition period rule: If land was transferred between December 1, 2018 and January 31, 2019, the new owner gets a proportionate amount for the first installment (calculated by days).

Who is NOT Eligible (Exclusions)
1. Institutional landholders
Corporate farms, trusts, agricultural institutions — not eligible. Scheme is for human family units only.
2. Constitutional and political post holders
Current or former: Ministers (Union + State), MPs (Lok Sabha + Rajya Sabha), MLAs, MLCs, Mayors of Municipal Corporations, Chairpersons of District Panchayats.
3. Government employees
All serving or retired employees of Central/State Government Ministries, Departments, PSEs (Public Sector Enterprises), and local body employees.
Exception: MTS (Multi-Tasking Staff), Class IV, and Group D employees ARE eligible despite being government employees.
4. Pensioners receiving ₹10,000/month or more
Any retired person drawing a monthly pension of ₹10,000 or above is excluded.
Exception: Retired MTS, Class IV, and Group D employees are NOT excluded by this pension rule — they remain eligible.
5. Income tax payers
Anyone who paid income tax in the previous assessment year is excluded.
6. Registered practicing professionals
Doctors, Engineers, Lawyers, Chartered Accountants (CAs), and Architects — excluded if registered with their professional regulatory body and actively practicing.

Required Documents

Aadhaar Card — Mandatory (non-negotiable)
Land ownership records — Khatauni / Khasra / Revenue Land Passbook (state-specific)
Bank passbook or account details (Account Number + IFSC code)
Active mobile number linked to Aadhaar

Upload formats accepted: .pdf and .jpg only
If Aadhaar is pending: Temporarily use Aadhaar enrollment number + Voter ID, but full Aadhaar is required for continued payments.

How to Apply
Option 1 — Online (self-registration):

Go to pmkisan.gov.in → Farmers Corner → New Farmer Registration
Select Rural or Urban farmer
Enter Aadhaar number + mobile number → OTP verification (if OTP fails, registration is blocked)
Fill details: State, District, Block, Village, Name, Gender, Category, Bank IFSC, Account Number
Upload land records, Aadhaar copy, Bank passbook (pdf or jpg)
Submit → goes to State/District/Block officials for verification

Option 2 — Via CSC (Common Service Centre):
Visit nearest CSC — operator helps complete registration. No internet or smartphone needed.
Option 3 — State bulk upload:
State government can upload village-wise verified farmer lists directly into the portal.

After Applying

Application goes to State Nodal Officer for verification against land and tax databases
Once approved, name is added to beneficiary list
First payment starts from the next active installment cycle after approval
Re-application lockout: Cannot reapply or modify the same application for 30 days after submission
Timeline to approval: Not fixed — depends on state government verification speed


Mandatory eKYC (for existing beneficiaries)
All registered farmers receiving payments must complete eKYC to continue receiving benefits.

OTP-based eKYC: Aadhaar must be linked to mobile number → complete on pmkisan.gov.in
Biometric eKYC: Visit nearest CSC → fingerprint or iris scan by operator


Check Status / Track Application

Go to pmkisan.gov.in → Beneficiary List or Know Your Status → enter Aadhaar number
Can also use AI chatbot "Kisan-eMitra" on the portal for real-time status


Update / Correct Details
If details are wrong (bank account, mobile number, name spelling):

Go to pmkisan.gov.in → Edit/Update Self Registration or Update Missing Information


Common Reasons for Rejection / Payment Failure

Aadhaar not seeded/linked to bank account for DBT
Name mismatch between Aadhaar and bank records
State government rejected land record details
Applicant found to be paying income tax (detected via automated database check)
Duplicate family registration detected (husband + wife both registered separately)


Important Tips for Rural Users

Name spelling on Aadhaar must exactly match bank account name
Must link Aadhaar to bank account via NPCI mapping — money will fail without this
Check status online using Aadhaar number before visiting block office
